How technology change the business world

Changes in technology affect businesses and business activity in a major way. Many businesses have adapted to new technologies and use these to shape and influence their future. Technologies have made elements of business such as manufacturing, communication, purchasing, sales and advertising easier and more effective for businesses.




Changes in technology have included:

       

1. Email - electronic mail enables written messages to be sent instantly to others, and files can be shared as

    attachments.




       

2. Mobile applications - it enables customers and businesses to conduct banking transactions anywhere




       

3. Websites - a page or group of pages containing written and visual information using various media,

     this allows businesses to communicate with customers and has enabled e-commerce.




       

4. Web conferencing - it helps businesses reach out to their participants, investors and associates in far

    locations. One of the example being use in Web conferencing is google meet and zoom.




       

5. Manufacturing - a variety of technological manufacturing techniques are commonly used,

    such as using robots to build cars.




        

6. Electric vehicles - it helps earn more money or save more money by reducing the expenses

    on transportation and petroleum
